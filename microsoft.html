Research Proposal

Research Title
"Interpretable AI for Healthcare Diagnostics: Bridging the Gap Between Accuracy and Explainability"
Research Abstract
This research proposes developing novel interpretable AI models for medical diagnostics that maintain high accuracy while providing transparent decision-making processes. The project will focus on creating hybrid neural-symbolic systems that combine the pattern recognition capabilities of deep learning with the logical reasoning of symbolic AI. This approach aims to address the critical need for trustworthy AI in healthcare settings where diagnostic decisions must be explainable to medical professionals and patients alike. The research will specifically target early detection of neurological disorders through multimodal data analysis.
